About Me

Hello, my name is I Gede Kasuma Dana, though most people call me Kasuma. I am a student specializing in Software Engineering at a vocational school in Denpasar. Born and raised in this vibrant city, I have always had a deep passion for technology. I also enjoy photography as a hobby, capturing interesting moments in life even if it remains just a pastime.

One of the most memorable experiences in my life took place when I was in the 2nd grade. I had the rare opportunity to perform at the Bali Arts Festival. Stepping onto the stage at the Art Center in Ardha Candra, together with my art group, Sanggar Drupadi, and Palawara—a music ensemble collaborating with our group—opened my eyes to the beauty of the arts and sparked a creativity that I continue to nurture.

However, not every chapter of my life has been filled with joy. Several years ago, I experienced profound sorrow when my great-grandparents passed away. This loss hit me hard, especially since my great-grandfather was a cherished figure who always accompanied me as I played musical instruments in our village. That experience taught me the importance of cherishing every moment with our loved ones.

Throughout my educational journey, I have actively participated in the Spensix Multimedia Computer Club, where I focused on honing my skills in Microsoft Word. During this time, I achieved several accomplishments, such as winning competitions at Alfa Prima and at a local high school event. I also proudly earned accolades in a quiz competition organized by FAD Denpasar and in an essay contest held by Bank BPD Bali. In the 8th grade, I was entrusted with the role of Microsoft Word coordinator and later served as Vice Chairman of the club. Perhaps, if I had not joined this organization, I might never have developed my interest in Software Engineering or pursued my current educational path.

Looking ahead, I aspire to continue developing my potential. After completing my vocational education, I plan to pursue higher studies in Informatics Engineering at Politeknik Negeri Bali. While in college, I also intend to take on a part-time job to gain further experience and broaden my horizons in the ever-evolving world of technology.
